How to use this unit converter

Select a category such as length, weight, temperature, or speed. Enter a value, choose the “From” and “To” units, then click Convert to see the result instantly.

Common conversion examples

  • Length: Convert centimeters to inches for screen sizes, furniture, or DIY projects.
  • Weight: Convert kilograms to pounds for shipping, fitness tracking, or luggage limits.
  • Temperature: Convert Celsius to Fahrenheit for recipes, weather, or travel.
  • Speed: Convert km/h to mph when driving or cycling overseas.

Who should use this tool?

This unit converter is useful for students, teachers, home cooks, online sellers, travelers, and professionals who need fast, reliable unit conversions for daily tasks.

Accuracy and limitations

Conversions are calculated using standard formulas and are suitable for general use. For scientific, medical, or regulatory purposes, always confirm values using official or industry-approved references.
