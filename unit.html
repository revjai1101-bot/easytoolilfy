How to use this unit converter

Choose a category (length, weight, temperature, or speed), enter a value, then select the “From” and “To” units. Tap Convert to get the result. This is useful when comparing product specs, measuring for DIY, or checking homework calculations.

Common examples

  • Length: cm to inch for screen sizes or product dimensions.
  • Weight: kg to lb for shipping or travel luggage limits.
  • Temperature: Celsius to Fahrenheit for travel and recipes.
  • Speed: km/h to mph for driving overseas.

Tips for accurate conversions

If you need precision (engineering, medicine, legal documents), double-check with an official reference. For daily use, rounding to 2–4 decimal places is typically enough.
