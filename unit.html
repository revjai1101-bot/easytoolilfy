How to use this unit converter

Select a category such as length, weight, temperature, or speed. Enter a value, choose the “From” and “To” units, then click Convert to see the result instantly.

Common examples

  • Length: Convert centimeters to inches for screen sizes or furniture measurements.
  • Weight: Convert kilograms to pounds for shipping, fitness tracking, or travel luggage.
  • Temperature: Convert Celsius to Fahrenheit when following recipes or planning trips.
  • Speed: Convert km/h to mph for driving or cycling comparisons.

Who is this tool for?

This unit converter is useful for students, home cooks, online sellers, travelers, and anyone who needs fast and reliable unit conversions without installing apps.

Accuracy and limitations

Results are calculated using standard conversion formulas and are intended for general use. For professional or regulatory purposes, always verify values using official references.
