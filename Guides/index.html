How to Create Strong & Secure Passwords

Strong passwords protect your online accounts from hacking, data leaks, and unauthorized access. Many users reuse weak passwords without realizing the risks.

A secure password should be:

  • At least 12 characters long
  • A mix of letters, numbers, and symbols
  • Unique for each website or service

The Password Generator on Toolify Hub creates strong passwords directly in your browser. No passwords are stored or sent to any server, making it safe for everyday use.

Using a password generator reduces the risk of brute-force attacks and password reuse.
