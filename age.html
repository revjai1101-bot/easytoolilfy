Age Calculator

Calculate your age quickly by entering your birthdate. Useful for forms, records, and personal reference.

How the age calculation works

The calculator compares your birthdate with the current date and calculates the number of full years that have passed.

When you might need an age calculator

  • Filling in official forms
  • School or exam registrations
  • Insurance or employment records
  • Personal milestones and planning

Accuracy notes

This tool calculates age in full years. Some institutions may require age in years and months — always check specific requirements.
