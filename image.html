Image Resizer

Resize images directly in your browser without uploading them to a server. This tool is ideal for profile photos, online forms, website uploads, and quick image adjustments.

How to resize an image

Upload an image from your device, enter the width and height you need, then click the Resize Image button. The resized image will be shown instantly, and you can download it with a single click.

Common reasons to resize images

  • Meeting image size requirements for online forms
  • Creating profile pictures for social media or work platforms
  • Optimizing images for websites and blogs
  • Reducing image dimensions for faster loading

Privacy and security

All image processing happens locally in your browser. Your images are never uploaded, stored, or shared with any server, making this tool suitable for personal and sensitive images.

Who should use this image resizer?

This tool is useful for students, professionals, content creators, online sellers, and anyone who needs to resize images quickly without installing software or signing up for an account.
