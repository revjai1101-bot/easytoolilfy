Image Resizer

Resize images directly in your browser without uploading them to a server. Ideal for profile photos, forms, and online submissions.

How to resize an image

Upload an image, enter the desired width and height, then click Resize. The resized image will appear below, and you can download it instantly.

Why resize images

Resizing images helps meet upload requirements, reduce file size, and ensure images display correctly on websites and applications.

Privacy note

All image processing happens locally in your browser. Your images are not uploaded or stored on any server.
