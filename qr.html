QR Code Generator

Create QR codes for links, text, or contact information. Generated QR codes can be downloaded and shared easily.

How to use this QR code generator

Enter any text or URL into the input field and click Generate. A QR code will appear instantly, which you can scan using a smartphone camera or QR scanner app.

Common uses for QR codes

  • Restaurant menus and table ordering
  • WiFi sharing
  • Business cards and resumes
  • Event tickets and invitations

Tips for using QR codes effectively

Make sure the QR code is printed clearly and large enough to scan. Test it before sharing publicly. Avoid placing QR codes in low-light or reflective areas.
