How to use this currency converter

Start by entering an amount, then choose the currency you have (“From”) and the currency you want (“To”). Tap Convert to see an estimated result. You can star currencies you use often to keep them at the top of the list.

  • Use whole amounts first (e.g., 100) to sanity-check the conversion.
  • For travel, compare the converted amount with your bank’s card rate or money changer rate.
  • If your internet is unstable, the tool may use fallback rates for reference.

Why exchange rates change

Exchange rates move due to supply and demand in global markets. Rates can shift when interest rates change, when inflation changes, or when major economic news affects investor confidence. Even without big news, rates may update throughout the day as liquidity changes.

Tips for getting better rates

For the best value, compare multiple sources: bank cards, remittance services, and physical money changers. Watch out for hidden fees, wide spreads, or poor “buy/sell” differences. If you exchange in cash, avoid changing at airports unless necessary.
