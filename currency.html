How to use this currency converter

Enter an amount, select the currency you have (“From”) and the currency you want (“To”), then click Convert to see an estimated value. You can star frequently used currencies so they appear at the top of the list.

  • Use round numbers first (e.g., 100) to quickly understand the conversion.
  • Compare the result with your bank card rate or remittance service.
  • If your connection is unstable, fallback rates may be used for reference.

Why exchange rates change

Exchange rates fluctuate due to supply and demand in global financial markets. Factors such as interest rates, inflation, trade activity, and economic news can cause rates to change throughout the day.

Tips for getting better exchange rates

To get the best value, compare multiple options including bank cards, online remittance platforms, and local money changers. Watch out for hidden fees and wide buy/sell spreads. When possible, avoid exchanging cash at airports.

Who is this tool useful for?

This currency converter is commonly used by travelers, online shoppers, freelancers working with international clients, and anyone comparing prices across different countries.
